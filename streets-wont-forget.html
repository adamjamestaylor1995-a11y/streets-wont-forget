How to Play
The Barclays Premier League Invitational Tournament is here - compete against the most iconic sides of that era, filled with players that the streets won't forget.

Create your club, hire a manager, pick your formation, and draft 7 players who can lead your team to glory.

Spend your budget wisely - run out of funds and you'll need to resort to free agents!

Strategy and a bit of luck will be crucial if you want to win silverware.

Every match plays out in real time. Win it all and etch your name into folklore.
